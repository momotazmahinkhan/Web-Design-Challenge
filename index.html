Its All About weather



The purpose of this project is to analyze how temperature, humidity, cloudiness and wind speed in the Northern and Southern Hemisphere weather. To accmpolish this analysis, the weather data was pulled from the OpenWeather API to gather dataset on over 500 cities.

After assembling the dataset, Matplotlib library was used to plot various aspects of the weather vs. latitude in the two hemispheres. The factors that were analyzed included: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed and humidity. The OpenWeather site provides the data and the visualizations were created as part of the analysis, as well as explanations and descriptions of any trends and correlations witnessed
